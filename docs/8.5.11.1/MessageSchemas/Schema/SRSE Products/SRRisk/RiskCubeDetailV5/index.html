RiskCubeDetailV5

V8 Message Definiton

RiskCubeDetail records are published by CoreRiskServers and are designed to be aggregated and analyzed over time. RiskCubeDetail records are published to the SpiderRock Archive Host data pipeline at the end of each trading period.
