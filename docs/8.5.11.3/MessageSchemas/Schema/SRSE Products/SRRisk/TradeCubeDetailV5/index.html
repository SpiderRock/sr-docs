TradeCubeDetailV5

V8 Message Definiton

TradeCubeDetail records are published by CoreRiskServers and are visible in SRSE throughout the day. They are designed to be aggregated and analyzed over time. TradeCubeDetail records are published to the SpiderRock Archive Host data pipeline after all markup detail is complete.
