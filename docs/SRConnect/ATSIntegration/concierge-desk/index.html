Concierge Desk

Overview​

The SpiderRock Concierge Desk operates as an agency broker dealer offering clients access to SpiderRock Connect services including ATS auctions and proprietary execution algorithms, in addition to traditional brokerage services.

This document will explain how to connect with the SpiderRock Concierge desk and what services the Spider Rock Concierge desk provides to Concierge Desk clients.

Contacting the SpiderRock Concierge Desk​

Desk Member Services​

The SpiderRock Concierge Desk is staffed with FINRA-licensed brokers capable of executing orders on behalf of any Concierge Desk client on an agency basis. Regular methods of communication with the desk include phone and chat services such as Bloomberg IB or ICE Chat.

Concierge Desk vs. Client Support Desk​

SpiderRock Connect offers a suite of tools for clients to interact directly within the SpiderRock ecosystem of auctions and liquidity, supported by a separate Client Support Desk to facilitate interactions and troubleshoot issues with these tools and transactions. The Concierge Desk also utilizes these tools as intermediary on behalf of Concierge Desk clients and provides support to Concierge Desk clients in facilitating transactions with SpiderRock liquidity.

Order and Auction Services​

Order Initiation​

New initiating orders can be communicated to the SpiderRock Concierge desk via approved communication channels including email, phone, Bloomberg / Bloomberg IB, ICE Chat, (Slack and Cloud9?). Order instructions can also be transmitted via FIX message or staged to the Concierge Desk from the SpiderRock Connect Trade App and via MLink.

Orders received by the Concierge Desk may be introduced by the Concierge Desk into SpiderRock Connect services such as ATS Auctions and proprietary execution algorithms. Those services include:

  • Flash Auction
  • Block Auction
  • SpiderRock Execution Algorithms

The SpiderRock Concierge Desk serves as the primary point of contact for all information relating to orders facilitated through the desk and will provide assistance as needed throughout the order lifecycle.

Auction Responses​

The SpiderRock Concierge Desk facilitates interaction between Concierge Desk clients and SpiderRock Connect ATS Auctions by hosting communication channels for automated ATS Auction messages via chat services such as ICE Chat. Automated messages will be delivered to these channels and responses will be ingested back into ATS auctions by an electronic participant in these chats. For more information on this workflow, refer to the “SRConnect ICE Chat Manual”. SpiderRock Concierge Desk employees are also present in all electronic communication channels enabled for distribution of automated Auction communications and capable of responding to inquiries relating to ATS Auction messages represented in these channels.

Note: The SpiderRock Concierge Desk facilitates access to SpiderRock Connect services. However, the Concierge Desk is independent from SpiderRock Connect and cannot interfere with the electronic operation of SpiderRock Connect services including ATS Auctions. Any assistance offered in connection with ATS Auction transactions is on a best-effort basis and cannot be interpreted to represent interaction with SpiderRock Connect ATS Auction transactions unless confirmed directly by communication from SpiderRock Connect.

Additional Services​

In addition to handling orders for Concierge Desk clients, the Concierge Desk can provide other services to assist with interacting with SpiderRock Connect liquidity through Concierge Desk channels. This includes services such as managing filters and interest lists.
