Stripes

MLink Stripes​

The SpiderRock system stripes its data in pre-defined sets of tickers (tk), indexes, or exchanges (ts). In the context of the MLink API, they may be used in order to either balance the load on the receiving end and/or segment (stripes correspond to different sets of assets). This is especially useful if you are trying to distribute the incoming message load, or are interested in tickers within specific stripes.
